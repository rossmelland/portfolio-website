When you want to turn your life around, you start by changing the way you dress. This TVC for Jet Man tells the true story of Bheki Kunene. Bheki was in prison — his life at an all-time low. When he was released, he faced a crossroad: return to the life he had before, or make himself into a Better Man. Today, Bheki is the CEO of his own design agency, employing dozens of people.
