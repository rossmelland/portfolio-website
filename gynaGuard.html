When working on this project, I was the only man on the team. We quickly discovered that discussing this topic made the women unsure or uncomfortable. That reluctance became our insight: how can you look after your vagina if you feel ashamed to speak about it?

South Africa has 12 official languages, and in most of them there is no official word for 'vagina'. The words commonly used are crude or simply euphemisms, making women feel it was taboo to even say them.

During August — South Africa's Women's Month — GynaGuard's campaign aimed to identify a respectful word for 'vagina' in all 12 official languages, removing the shame and empowering women to take care of their health.
