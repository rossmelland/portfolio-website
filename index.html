I'm Ross — a Senior Art Director with 15 years of experience across advertising and licensed product development. I've led campaigns at Joe Public United in South Africa and spent five years at WildBrain CPLG in Amsterdam managing creative across 15+ franchises, 60+ licensees, and 15+ European markets.

My work ranges from pan-African brand campaigns to globally distributed licensed products. I bring the same thinking to both — strong concept, considered craft, and a clear understanding of what makes an idea work in the real world.

Based in Amsterdam. Available for freelance projects, creative direction, and consultancy.
