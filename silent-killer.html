Silent Killer

Client
Heart and Stroke Foundation South Africa
Category
TVC
Year
2009
Awards
Gold, Best Cinematography, VUKA Awards 2009

When obesity surpassed smoking as the number one cause of heart disease in South Africa, most people still didn't see it as the bigger threat. At the same time, public smoking restrictions were being tightened across the country. The idea was simple: there's a devil we know, and a devil we don't. Silent Killer put the unseen danger in plain sight.

DOP — Warren Smart
