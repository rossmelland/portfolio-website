LG Ziyawa

Client
LG
Category
Social Media
Year
2013

South Africa has one of the highest mobile user populations in the world, but most devices are older models with poor speakers. When LG briefed us on showcasing their new Ziyawa speakers online, we faced a challenge: how do you demonstrate great sound on a device that can't reproduce it? The answer was visual. We filled the speakers with paint, glitter and powder and filmed it all exploding out — letting people see the music instead of hear it.
